ResponSlider.js

ResponSlider.js showcase

Here are some examples where you can see how our brand-new Responsive Slider works.

Example 1

Let's build a simple slider with 3 visible slides, and 1 moving slide every time we click on a previous or next button. Here's the code:

new ResponSlider("#slider", {
  movingSlides: 1,
  visibleSlides: 3,
  previousSlideAction: "#previousSlide",    
  nextSlideAction: "#nextSlide"   
});

Here's the actual slider:

Previous slide Next slide

Resize your browser window so you can see how the slider adatps to its container width.

Example 2

Now, we can create a slider with 2 visible slides, that moves 2 slides on every click. Slides should be horizontally and vertically centered.

new ResponSlider("#slider2", {
  movingSlides: 2,
  visibleSlides: 2,
  previousSlideAction: "#previousSlide2",    
  nextSlideAction: "#nextSlide2",
  horizontallyCentered: true,
  verticallyCentered: true   
});
Previous slide Next slide

If you resize your browser window, you can see that images always stay in the center, even if they are taller than the slider height
